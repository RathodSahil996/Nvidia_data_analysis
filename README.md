# Nvidia_data_analysis
# 🚀 NVIDIA 2026 Global Sales Analytics: My Debut Data Project

> **A Note on this Repository:** This is my very first end-to-end data analytics portfolio project! I took a raw dataset of over 7,000 synthetic transactions and transformed it into actionable business intelligence using advanced Excel techniques and Power BI interactive dashboards. 

## 📌 Project Overview
The objective of this project was to analyze NVIDIA's synthetic 2026 global sales data to uncover insights regarding product volume, revenue generation, regional market share, and the impact of supply chain scarcity on consumer pricing. 

This project explores the massive shift in NVIDIA's business model from Consumer Gaming GPUs to Enterprise Data Center AI infrastructure.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
* **Microsoft Excel:** Data cleaning, Pivot Tables, logical formulas (`=IF`), and advanced data modeling (Global Pareto 80/20 Analysis).
* **Power BI:** Interactive data visualization, time-series forecasting, and conditional formatting.

## 📊 Key Business Insights Discovered

### Part 1: Excel Analytics
1. **Volume vs. Value:** Demonstrated that while consumer cards (like the RTX 5070) drive the highest unit volume, enterprise AI chips (like the B200) generate the overwhelming majority of global revenue.
2. **The 80/20 Rule (Pareto Analysis):** Built a flattened global running total using a custom Helper Column to prove that just a small fraction of Region-Segment combinations (e.g., North America - Gaming, Europe - Hyperscale Datacenter) generate 80% of the company's wealth.
3. **Warranty Operations:** Discovered that while Extended Warranties sell the most volume, dedicated Support Contracts yield the highest customer satisfaction scores (4.62/5).

### Part 2: Power BI Interactive Dashboards
1. **Pricing & Supply Dynamics (Scatter Plot):** Visualized the relationship between MSRP and Street Price, using a stock status slicer to prove how inventory scarcity ("Sold Out") drastically drives up consumer markups.
2. **The Enterprise AI Boom (Time-Series Line Chart):** Tracked quarterly revenue growth, clearly showing the Data Center AI segment completely overtaking Consumer Gaming revenue as the year progressed.
3. **Customer Segment Matrix (Heatmap):** Utilized conditional background formatting to pinpoint Direct Enterprise sales to Cloud Providers as the absolute highest-earning sales channel.
